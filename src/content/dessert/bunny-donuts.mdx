{<p><strong>WHAT YOU'LL NEED</strong><br />20 mini donuts<br />8 ounces <a target="_blank" href="https://www.amazon.com/Wilton-1911-1300-Candy-Melts-Bright/dp/B00HE5YK2I/ref=sr_1_1?ie=UTF8&amp;qid=1489835610&amp;sr=8-1&amp;keywords=white+candy+melts">bright white candy melts</a><br />2 ounces <a target="_blank" href="https://www.amazon.com/Wilton-1-Round-Decorating-Tip/dp/B004AT3XCO">black candy melts</a><br />2 ounces <a target="_blank" href="https://www.amazon.com/Wilton-12-Ounce-1911-1361-Discontinued-Manufacturer/dp/B0061TXSSA/ref=sr_1_1?ie=UTF8&amp;qid=1489836110&amp;sr=8-1&amp;keywords=pink+candy+melts">pink candy melts</a><br /><a target="_blank" href="https://www.amazon.com/Viva-Naturals-Organic-Virgin-Coconut/dp/B00DS842HS/ref=sr_1_3_a_it?ie=UTF8&amp;qid=1489836166&amp;sr=8-3&amp;keywords=coconut+oil">coconut oil</a><br /><a target="_blank" href="https://www.amazon.com/Top-Rated-Bellemain-Cooling-Rack/dp/B00OY91N90/ref=sr_1_6?s=kitchen&amp;ie=UTF8&amp;qid=1489836206&amp;sr=1-6&amp;keywords=cooling+rack">cooling rack</a><br /><a target="_blank" href="https://www.amazon.com/Reynolds-Premium-Parchment-Non-Stick-Square/dp/B0166SPEL4/ref=sr_1_6?ie=UTF8&amp;qid=1489836244&amp;sr=1-6&amp;keywords=parchment+paper">parchment paper</a><br /><a href="https://www.amazon.com/Jet-Puffed-Miniature-Marshmallows-16/dp/B000PKJB6Y/ref=sr_1_2_a_it?ie=UTF8&amp;qid=1489836274&amp;sr=8-2&amp;keywords=mini+marshmallows">10 mini marshmallows</a><br /><a target="_blank" href="https://www.amazon.com/Wilton-Decorating-Tip-No-2-Round/dp/B0043YRPXY/ref=sr_1_1?ie=UTF8&amp;qid=1489835647&amp;sr=8-1&amp;keywords=wilton+%232+piping+tip">#2 piping tip</a><br /><a target="_blank" href="https://www.amazon.com/Wilton-1-Round-Decorating-Tip/dp/B004AT3XCO">#1 piping tip</a><br /><a href="https://www.amazon.com/Ateco-Disposable-Pastry-Bags-12-Inch/dp/B01EXHJCTA/ref=sr_1_9?ie=UTF8&amp;qid=1489836296&amp;sr=8-9&amp;keywords=piping+bags">piping bags</a></p>}{<p>Once you have your donuts, take your bright white candy melts and melt per instructions. Add a little coconut oil, like a pea size amount. It makes the candy melts a little thinner and easier to coat evenly and smooth. You can add a little more coconut oil if you don't think that was enough. Once you have your candy melts at the right consistency, dip your donuts in halfway and place them on a cooling rack over parchment paper.</p>}
{<p><Picture src={ContentImage2} alt="Bunny Donuts | Sarah Makes Stuff"/></p>}
{<p>Then take your mini marshmallows and cut them half with scissors that you have dipped in water. The water makes it a lot easier to cut. Place them on the donuts before they have dried.</p>}
{<p><Picture src={ContentImage3} alt="Bunny Donuts | Sarah Makes Stuff"/></p>}
{<p>Then once dried, use the same white candy melts (you may have to reheat) in a piping bag fitted with a #2 piping tip<a href="#">
</a>to make the little ears.</p>}
{<p><Picture src={ContentImage4} alt="Bunny Donuts | Sarah Makes Stuff"/></p>}
{<p>Then melt a little bit of
black candy melts, and in a piping bag fitted with a #1 piping tip,
pipe on the eyes. And lastly, with melted pink candy melts in a piping bag with a #1 tip, pipe on the the pink inside of the ears on top of the white ears you made, and the little nose.</p>}
