<div class="sqs-html-content">
	<p>
		<strong>MACARON SHELL INGREDIENTS</strong>
		<br />
		<a
			target="_blank"
			href="http://www.sarahmakesstuff.com/dessert/2016/8/14/orange-white-chocolate-macarons"
		>
			See this post
		</a>
		<strong> </strong>
	</p>
	<p>
		<strong>CIRCUS ANIMAL GANACHE INGREDIENTS</strong>
		<br />
		<span>227 grams white chocolate, good quality, not chips</span>
		<br />
		<span>115 grams heavy cream</span>
		<br />
		<span>16 Circus Animal Cookies</span>
	</p>
	<p>
		<strong>MACARON SHELL INSTRUCTIONS</strong>
		<br />
		Follow instructions from{' '}
		<a
			target="_blank"
			href="http://www.sarahmakesstuff.com/dessert/2016/8/14/orange-white-chocolate-macarons"
		>
			this recipe
		</a>
		, except you can either make two batches of batter, adding{' '}
		<a
			target="_blank"
			href="https://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B007EMYD8M/ref=oh_aui_detailpage_o02_s01?ie=UTF8&amp;psc=1"
		>
			magenta gel food coloring
		</a>
		 to one, or you can separate the batter at the
		add-meringue-and-food-coloring stage and add the food coloring to one of the
		halves. Make sure you also sprinkle on some nonpareils sprinkles onto each
		shell before setting out to dry.
	</p>
	<p>
		<strong>CIRCUS ANIMAL MACARON INSTRUCTIONS</strong>
		<br />
		In a food processor, blend your circus animal cookies until smooth.
	</p>
	<p>
		Finely chop white chocolate and place in a small bowl. Set aside. In a small
		heavy bottomed pot, heat heavy cream until boiling, the edges should be
		bubbling. Pour heavy cream on top of the finely chopped white chocolate and
		immediately cover. Don't stir! Let sit for two minutes covered. After two
		minutes, stir it smooth. If all the pieces aren't melted you can set the
		bowl onto a pot of boiling water and stir until smooth.
	</p>
	<p>
		Add your finely ground animal circus cookies to the ganache while ganache is
		still warm. Mix with a fork until smooth. Once smooth, put it in the fridge
		to set. It will get too hard if you leave it in there too long, but if that
		happens you can just set it out until it warms up a little. 
	</p>
	<p>
		Once set, place your ganache in a piping bag. Match up pink macaron shells
		with white macaron shells of similar shape and size. Pipe out some ganache
		onto one side and gently twist together. 
	</p>
	<p>
		Put in fridge for at least 8 hours. Let come to room temperature before
		enjoying.
	</p>
