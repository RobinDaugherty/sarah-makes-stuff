<div class="sqs-html-content">
	<p>
		<strong>INGREDIENTS </strong>
		<em>serves 10</em>
		<br />1{' '}
		<a
			target="_blank"
			href="https://www.amazon.com/Amscan-Miniature-Charms-Plastic-Shower/dp/B000R4O9MY/ref=sr_1_5?ie=UTF8&amp;qid=1503779262&amp;sr=8-5&amp;keywords=king+cake+plastic+babies"
		>
			miniature plastic baby
		</a>
		<br />
		<a
			target="_blank"
			href="https://www.amazon.com/Uni-posca-Paint-Marker-Pen-PC-5M15C/dp/B001ANVDMU/ref=sr_1_4?ie=UTF8&amp;qid=1503779367&amp;sr=8-4&amp;keywords=posca+blue+paint+pen"
		>
			light blue paint pen
		</a>
		<br />
		85 grams (6 tbs) unsalted butter, melted
		<br />
		50 grams (1/4 cup) light brown sugar
		<br />
		12 grams (2 tsp) cinnamon
		<br />2 17.5 ounce cans jumbo cinnamon rolls with icing
		<br />
		black, medium blue, and light blue sprinkles
	</p>
	<p>
		<strong>INSTRUCTIONS</strong>
	</p>
	<ol>
		<li>
			Dot blue eyes over the eyes of the miniature plastic baby with the blue
			paint pen. 
		</li>
		<li>Preheat oven to 350°F.</li>
		<li>Evenly brush bundt pan with melted butter.</li>
		<li>
			Mix together brown sugar and cinnamon. Evenly coat buttered bundt pan with
			cinnamon-sugar mixture.
		</li>
		<li>
			Arrange cinnamon rolls around the prepared pan, overlapping the pieces
			slightly over each other. Pour over the remaining butter.
		</li>
		<li>Tuck the baby into one of the cinnamon rolls.</li>
		<li>
			Bake for 20-30 minutes, or until golden. Let cool for 5 minutes, then
			invert onto a cake plate.
		</li>
		<li>Pour frosting over the top and decorate with sprinkles.</li>
	</ol>
