<div class="sqs-html-content">
	<p>
		<strong>INGREDIENTS</strong>
		<br />
		341 grams (1 ½ cup) butter
		<br />
		327 grams (1 ½ cup) brown sugar
		<br />
		198 grams (1 cup) granulated sugar
		<br />½ cup milk
		<br />2 tsp vanilla
		<br />
		563 grams (4 ½ cup) all-purpose flour,{' '}
		<a
			target="_blank"
			href="http://www.cookinglight.com/eating-smart/smart-choices/safe-to-eat-cookie-dough"
		>
			toasted
		</a>
		 <br />1 12 ounce bag circus animal cookies, crushed
		<br />
		<em>optional, additional bag of cookies for decorating and scooping</em>
	</p>
	<p>
		<strong>INSTRUCTIONS</strong>
	</p>
	<ol dir="ltr">
		<li>
			In a large bowl of an electric mixer, fitted with a paddle attachment,
			beat butter and sugars together on medium speed until light and fluffy, 2
			to 3 minutes.
		</li>
		<li>Add milk and vanilla and continue beating until combined.</li>
		<li>Reduce the speed to low, add flour, and beat until incorporated.</li>
		<li>Stir in crushed circus animal cookies.</li>
		<li>
			The cookie dough can be consumed immediately or covered and stashed in an
			airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days.
		</li>
	</ol>
	<p>
		<strong>NUTRITION (25 servings)</strong>
		<br />
		calories: 329, total fat: 14 g, saturated fat: 10 g, trans fat: 0 g,
		cholesterol: 29 mg, sodium: 121 mg, potassium: 46 mg, total carbohydrate: 47
		g, dietary fiber: 1 g, sugars: 27 g, protein: 3 g
	</p>
