<div class="sqs-html-content">
	<p>
		<strong>INGREDIENTS </strong>
		<em>makes 32 mini rolls</em>
		<br />2 8 ounce packages crescent roll dough
		<br />
		57 grams (4 tbs) butter, melted
		<br />7 ounces (half a bag) Circus Animal Cookies
		<br />
		180 grams (1 ½ cups) powdered sugar
		<br />4 grams (1 tsp) vanilla
		<br />
		2-4 tbs milk
	</p>
	<p>
		<strong>INSTRUCTIONS</strong>
	</p>
	<ol dir="ltr">
		<li>
			Preheat oven according to crescent roll dough package directions. Lightly
			grease mini muffin pan. 
		</li>
		<li>
			Unroll crescent roll dough. Take half the dough, a square of four
			triangles, and place on a floured surface. Pinch the perforations together
			on both sides. Using a floured rolling pin, smooth out dough. 
		</li>
		<li>
			Brush on 1 tablespoon, about a fourth, of the melted butter. Sprinkle on a
			thin, even layer of circus animal cookie crumbs. Tightly roll into a log,
			trim ends, and cut into eight rounds. 
		</li>
		<li>
			Place mini rolls into your prepared mini muffin pan. Repeat with the rest
			of the crescent roll dough. Bake according to package directions. You may
			have to do more than one batch depending on the size of your mini muffin
			pan.
		</li>
		<li>
			Meanwhile, whisk together powdered sugar with vanilla extract and milk,
			adding milk 1 tablespoon at a time, until consistency is reached. You want
			it thick, but not globby. Then whisk in ⅛ cup circus animal cookie crumbs.
			Add more milk if necessary to reach desired consistency. Drizzle over warm
			circus animal cookie rolls. 
		</li>
		<li>Eat right away, or store in an airtight container for up to 2 days.</li>
	</ol>
