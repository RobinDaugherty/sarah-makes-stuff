<div class="sqs-html-content">
	<p>
		<strong>DONUT INGREDIENTS</strong>
		<br />
		140 grams (1 cup) all-purpose flour
		<br />
		100 grams (1/2 cup) granulated sugar
		<br />1 tbsp baking powder
		<br />1 egg
		<br />
		115 grams (1/2 cup) milk
		<br />
		1/2 tsp vanilla
		<br />4 tbsp vegetable oil
	</p>
	<p>
		<strong>CHURRO TOPPING INGREDIENTS</strong>
		<br />
		57 grams (¼ cup) butter, melted
		<br />
		100 grams (1/2 cup) granulated sugar
		<br />1 tbsp cinnamon
	</p>
	<p>
		<strong>CHOCOLATE SAUCE INGREDIENTS</strong>
		<br />3 tsp corn starch
		<br />
		288 grams (1¼ cup) milk
		<br />
		50 grams chocolate
		<br />
		sugar, to taste
	</p>
	<p>
		<strong>DONUT INSTRUCTIONS</strong>
		<br />
		Sift dry ingredients together. Add egg, vanilla, and milk and beat 1 minute
		with electric mixer.
	</p>
	<p>Add oil and continue to beat 1 minute more.</p>
	<p>
		<em>If using a donut maker,</em> fill and bake donuts according to your
		donut maker.{' '}
		<a
			target="_blank"
			href="https://www.amazon.com/Holiday-Time-Mini-Donut-Maker/dp/B008FCF7W6"
		>
			This is the donut maker I have
		</a>
		. It was a cheap, impulse buy around Christmas a couple years ago. I'm SURE
		there are better ones out there. 
	</p>
	<p>
		<em>If using a donut baking tin,</em> spoon the batter into baking tin.
		Place in oven preheated to 350 F and bake for 8-12 minutes, or until a
		toothpick inserted into donut comes out clean. Take out of the oven and
		allow to cool in the pan for 5 minutes before taking out onto a wire rack to
		cool completely.
	</p>
	<p>
		<strong>CHURRO TOPPING INSTRUCTIONS</strong>
		<br />
		In a small bowl, melt butter. In another small bowl, mix sugar and cinnamon.
		Brush each doughnut with butter and sprinkle with (or roll in)
		sugar-cinnamon mixture.
	</p>
	<p>
		<strong>CHOCOLATE SAUCE INSTRUCTIONS</strong>
		<br />
		Combine corn starch with some milk. In a saucepan, heat milk and chocolate
		until chocolate is melted. Add cornflour mixture gradually, cooking over a
		low heat. Stir constantly until the mixture thickens. Sweeten to taste.
	</p>
