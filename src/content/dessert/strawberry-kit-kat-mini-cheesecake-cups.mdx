<div class="sqs-html-content">
	<p>
		<strong>STRAWBERRY SUGAR COOKIE CUPS INGREDIENTS</strong> 
		<em>makes 24 mini cheesecake cups</em>
		<br />
		85 grams (6 tbs) unsalted butter, room temperature
		<br />
		99 grams (½ cup) granulated sugar
		<br />1 egg, room temperature
		<br />1 tbs{' '}
		<a
			target="_blank"
			href="https://onlinestore.smucker.com/display_product.cfm?prod_id=2737&amp;cat_id=145"
		>
			strawberry syrup
		</a>
		<br />
		156 grams (1¼ cups) all-purpose flour
		<br />
		2.5 grams (½ tsp) baking powder
		<br />
		pink gel food coloring
		<br />1 8 ounce bag (226 grams) 
		<a
			target="_blank"
			href="http://www.shopfoodex.com/hersheys-minis-unwrapped-flavors-california-crisp-wafers-strawberry-creme-pouch-p-62388.html?utm_source=Google&amp;utm_medium=Merchant%2BCenter&amp;utm_campaign=Feed&amp;gclid=EAIaIQobChMIucydsK241QIVmYuzCh1AeQVgEAkYAiABEgIp_PD_BwE"
		>
			Strawberry Kit Kat Minis
		</a>
		<br />
		85 grams (½ cup) white chocolate
	</p>
	<p>
		<strong>CHEESECAKE FILLING INGREDIENTS</strong>
		<br />
		227 grams (8 ounces) cream cheese, room temperature
		<br />
		90 grams (¾ cup) powdered sugar
		<br />2 tbs strawberry syrup
		<br />
		8.4 ounces (1 cup) heavy cream
	</p>
	<p>
		<strong>INSTRUCTIONS</strong>
	</p>
	<ol>
		<li>Preheat oven to 350°F. Grease a mini muffin pan.</li>
		<li>
			In the bowl of an electric mixer fitted with the paddle attachment, cream
			butter and sugar until light and creamy.
		</li>
		<li>
			Add egg, strawberry syrup, and enough gel food coloring to get the color
			you want (start small and build up) and continue to beat until
			incorporated.
		</li>
		<li>
			In a separate bowl, whisk together flour, baking powder, and salt. Add to
			wet ingredients and mix until well combined.
		</li>
		<li>Roll dough into 1" balls and place in prepared mini muffin pan.</li>
		<li>Bake at 350° for 9-11 minutes.</li>
		<li>
			Immediately after removing from oven, use the back of a round teaspoon to
			press the dough down in the middle, forming a cup. Once cool enough to
			touch, remove from pan and place on a cooling rack to cool completely.
		</li>
		<li>
			Meanwhile, take all but 24 of the mini Kit Kats and place in a food
			processor. Blend until you have fine crumbs. Place crumbs in small bowl.
		</li>
		<li>
			Melt your white chocolate per instructions on bag. Place in small bowl.
			Add a <em>tiny</em> bit of coconut oil if it needs thinning.
		</li>
		<li>
			Once the cookie cups have completely cooled, dip each cookie cup into the
			white chocolate, about half way, and immediately dip in Kit Kat crumbs.
			Place on parchment paper to harden. 
		</li>
		<li>
			Pipe a swirl of cheesecake filling (recipe below) into each cup and top
			with mini Kit Kats.
			<br />
			These will stay good for a couple days in an airtight container in the
			fridge.
		</li>
	</ol>
	<p>
		<strong>CHEESECAKE FILLING INSTRUCTIONS</strong>
	</p>
	<ol>
		<li>
			In the bowl of an electric mixer fitted with a paddle attachment, beat
			cream cheese on high speed until smooth.
		</li>
		<li>
			Add powdered sugar and vanilla and continue to beat until well
			incorporated. Scoop out into a separate bowl.
		</li>
		<li>
			Now in the bowl of your electric mixer fitted with a whisk attachment,
			whisk heavy cream until soft peaks form.
		</li>
		<li>
			Add the strawberry syrup and continue mixing until stiff peaks form.
		</li>
		<li>
			Fold in half of whipped cream into the cream cheese mixture. Repeat with
			the remaining whipped cream. Place in piping bag fitted with a large star
			tip and refrigerate until ready to pipe onto cookie cups.
			<br />
			<br />
			<strong>NUTRITION (24 cheesecake cups)</strong>
			<br />
			calories: 223, total fat: 14 g, saturated fat: 30 g, cholesterol: 39 mg,
			sodium: 77 mg, potassium: 16 mg, total carbohydrate: 23 g, dietary fiber:
			0 g, sugars: 16 g, protein: 2 g
		</li>
	</ol>
