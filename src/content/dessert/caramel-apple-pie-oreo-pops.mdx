		<br />
		<strong>INSTRUCTIONS</strong>
	</p>
	<ol>
		<li>
			Blend Apple Pie Oreos in a food processor until fine crumbs are achieved. 
		</li>
		<li>
			In the bowl of a stand mixer fitted with a paddle attachment, blend
			together Oreo crumbs, cream cheese, and caramel sauce. 
		</li>
		<li>Place in freezer for 10 minutes. </li>
		<li>Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.</li>
		<li>Roll Oreo mixture into 1.5 inch balls and place on prepared sheet. </li>
		<li>Place in freezer for 15 minutes. </li>
		<li>
			Melt red and green candy melts in microwave-safe bowls per instructions on
			package. Dip the tip of your lollipop stick into the melted candy coating,
			and insert into an Oreo ball, pushing it no more than halfway through.
			Holding the lollipop stick, dip the entire Oreo ball in the melted candy
			coating until it is completely covered. Place back on prepared sheet to
			harden. Repeat until all the Oreo balls are covered in red or green candy
			coating. Set aside to harden.
		</li>
		<li>
			Once completely hardened, melt white, light cocoa, and salted caramel
			candy melts in microwave-safe bowls per instructions on package. Dip red
			and green coated Oreo balls in the white, light cocoa, and salted caramel
			melted candy coating and sprinkle with crushed nuts, oreos, and sprinkles.
			Set back on prepared sheet to harden.
		</li>
	</ol>
