		if you find the right one it speaks to you and you have to have it. After

		I got to thinking that it would be super cute to continue the tea theme and
		make them into little mugs with tea-themed witty things written on them.
		This could also be done with espresso brownies with coffee-themed sayings.
		It depends what your cup of tea (or coffee) is.
	</p>

		First you need to make a batch of London Fog Blondies (or whatever iced
		blondie or brownie recipe you want if you're not a stickler about the theme
		part). Then take a look on Pinterest or Etsy and get some ideas for what you
		want to write on the mugs. 
	</p>
