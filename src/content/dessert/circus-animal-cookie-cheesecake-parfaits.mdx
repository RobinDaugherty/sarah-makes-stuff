<div class="sqs-html-content">
	<p>
		<strong>INGREDIENTS</strong>
		<br />
		476 grams (2 cups) whipping cream
		<br />
		452 grams (2 8 ounce packages) cream cheese, room temperature
		<br />
		50 grams (1/4 cup) granulated  sugar
		<br />1 tsp clear vanilla extract
		<br />2 tbs milk
		<br />
		neon pink gel food coloring
		<br />
		45 grams (3 tbs) caster sugar
		<br />
		396 grams (1 14 ounce bag) Circus Animal Cookies, crushed (reserve 4 cookies
		for toppers)
		<br />
		rainbow nonpareils sprinkles
	</p>
	<p>
		<strong>INSTRUCTIONS</strong>
	</p>
	<ol>
		<li>
			<p>
				In the bowl of an electric mixer fitted with a whisk attachment, whisk
				whipping cream and caster sugar until stiff peaks. Place in a separate
				bowl.
			</p>
		</li>
		<li>
			<p>
				In the bowl of an electric mixer fitted with a paddle attachment, beat
				together cream cheese, sugar, and vanilla until blended. 
			</p>
		</li>
		<li>
			<p>
				Divide in half. Add pink gel food coloring to one half until desired
				color is reached. 
			</p>
		</li>
		<li>
			<p>
				Fold in 1/3 of the whipped cream into each of the cream cheese mixtures.
			</p>
		</li>
		<li>
			<p>
				Place each of the cream cheese mixtures and the remaining whipped cream
				into piping bags fitted with large piping tips. I used a large star tip
				for the whipped cream.
			</p>
		</li>
		<li>
			<p>
				Layer white cream cheese mixer, crushed cookies, pink cream cheese
				mixture, and crushed cookies again into 4 serving glasses. Top with
				whipped cream, rainbow sprinkles and non-crushed animal cookie. 
			</p>
		</li>
	</ol>
	<p>
		<strong>NUTRITION (4 parfaits)</strong>
		<br />
		calories: 1176, total fat: 95 g, saturated fat: 66 g, monosaturated fat: 13
		g, cholesterol: 304 mg, sodium: 587 mg, potassium: 171 mg, total
		carbohydrate: 63 g, dietary fiber: 0 g, sugars: 48 g, protein: 13 g
	</p>
