{<p>
<strong>INGREDIENTS</strong>
<br />
<em>makes approximately 6 jars</em></p>}
{<p>
227 grams or 8 ounce package cream cheese, room temperature
<br />
270 grams (1 cup) creamy peanut butter
<br />
110 grams (1 cup) powdered sugar
<br />1 teaspoons vanilla extract
<br />
227 grams or 8 ounces whipped cream (I was lazy and just bought a can)
<br />
100 grams (3/4 cup) graham cracker crumbs
<br />
55 grams (2 tablespoons) melted butter
<br />
658 grams or 1.5 8 ounce bags mini peanut butter cups, chopped in fourths</p>}
{<p>
<strong>INSTRUCTIONS</strong>
<br />
In the bowl of an electric mixer fitted with the paddle attachment, combine
cream cheese, peanut butter and powdered sugar until smooth.</p>}
{<p>
Mix butter into the graham cracker crumbs with a fork. Add 3 tablespoons of
graham cracker crumb mixture to the bottom of the jars.</p>}
{<p>
Then make 2 layers of peanut butter filling, whipped cream and mini peanut
butter cups. I found it easiest to pipe both the peanut butter filling and
the whipped cream.</p>}
