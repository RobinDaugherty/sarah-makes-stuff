<div class="sqs-html-content">
	<p>
		<strong>INGREDIENTS</strong>
		<br />
		134 grams white chocolate chips
		<br />
		134 grams milk chocolate chips
		<br />
		134 grams dark chocolate chips
		<br />
		200 grams unsalted butter, room temperature
		<br />
		300 grams granulated sugar
		<br />1 large egg
		<br />
		325 grams self-rising sugar
		<br /> 
	</p>
	<p>
		<strong>INSTRUCTIONS</strong>
		<br />
		 Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Line cookie sheet with parchment paper or
		silicone mat.
	</p>
	<p>
		Using an electric mixer, cream the butter and sugar until pale and fluffy.
		Add the egg and continue to beat until smooth.
	</p>
	<p>
		Gradually add the flour and mix until well combined. Stir in chocolate
		chips. 
	</p>
	<p>
		Using a cookie scoop (or eye-balling it) roll dough into 2-inch balls and
		place on prepared pans, about 2 inches apart. Bake for 10-12 minutes.
		They'll still be gooey, but they'll set when they cool down. Transfer to a
		wire rack to cool.
	</p>
