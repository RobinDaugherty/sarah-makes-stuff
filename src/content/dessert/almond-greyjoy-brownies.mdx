<div class="sqs-html-content">
	<p>
		<strong>BROWNIE INGREDIENTS </strong>(<em>makes 9 brownies)</em>
		<br />
		227 grams (1 cup) butter, melted
		<br />
		396 grams (2 cups) granulated sugar
		<br />8 grams (2 tsp) vanilla extract
		<br />4 large eggs, about 200 grams
		<br />1 gram (1 tsp) instant espresso powder
		<br />
		125 grams (1 cup) all-purpose flour
		<br />
		62 grams (¾ cup) cocoa powder + extra for dusting
		<br />
		2.5 grams (½ tsp) baking powder
		<br />
		1.5 grams (¼ tsp) salt
		<br />8 snack size Almond Joys (4.8 ounces), roughly chopped
	</p>
	<p>
		<strong>COCONUT FROSTING INGREDIENTS</strong>
		<br />
		137 grams (⅓ cup) unsalted butter, room temperature
		<br />
		74 grams (¼ cup) unsweetened canned coconut cream
		<br />
		300 grams (2 ½ cups) powdered sugar
		<br />2 grams (½ tsp) vanilla extract
		<br />
		1.5 grams (¼ tsp) salt
		<br />
		60 grams (1 cup) unsweetened shredded coconut
	</p>
	<p>
		<strong>BROWNIE INSTRUCTIONS</strong>
	</p>
	<ol>
		<li>Preheat oven to 350°F. Line a 9x9 brownie pan with parchment paper.</li>
		<li>
			Whisk instant espresso powder and vanilla extract into melted butter.
		</li>
		<li>Whisk sugar into butter mixture until incorporated.</li>
		<li>Add eggs, one at a time, to butter/sugar mixture, mixing well. </li>
		<li>
			In a separate bowl, sift together flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, and
			salt.
		</li>
		<li>Add dry ingredients to wet ingredients and mix until incorporated.</li>
		<li>Stir in chopped Almond Joys.</li>
		<li>
			Pour batter into prepared pan. Bake 40 to 45 minutes or until cake tester
			comes out clean, or with just a few crumbs.
		</li>
		<li>
			Cool completely in pan on wire rack. Meanwhile, make Coconut Frosting,{' '}
			<em>recipe below.</em>
		</li>
		<li>
			Once completely cooled, take out of pan, add a layer of Coconut Frosting
			to the top, cut into nine squares and dust on krakens,{' '}
			<em>instructions below.</em>
		</li>
	</ol>
	<p>
		<strong>COCONUT FROSTING INSTRUCTIONS</strong>
	</p>
	<ol>
		<li>
			In the bowl of an electric mixer, cream together butter and coconut cream.
		</li>
		<li>Gradually add powdered sugar, beating until combined.</li>
		<li>
			Add vanilla extract and salt, increase speed to high, and beat until
			smooth and creamy.
		</li>
		<li>Add shredded coconut and continue to mix until well combined.</li>
	</ol>
	<p>
		<strong>COCOA KRAKENS</strong>
	</p>
	<ol>
		<li>
			Print out{' '}
			<a target="_blank" href="/s/Greyjoy-Kraken-646p.pdf">
				kraken graphic
			</a>
			.
		</li>
		<li>
			Cut out kraken with an exacto knife. You're going to be using it as a
			stencil, so the black parts don't matter, you want to pay attention the
			outline left behind.
		</li>
		<li>
			Trim your newly made stencil to about the size of one of your brownie
			squares. Lay on top of brownie, making sure it's fully in contact with the
			frosting.
		</li>
		<li>Dust with cocoa powder using a fine sieve.</li>
		<li>Gently remove the paper and repeat with the rest of your brownies. </li>
	</ol>
	<p>
