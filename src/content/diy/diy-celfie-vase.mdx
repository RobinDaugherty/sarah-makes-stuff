		This was so simple to make. Since the vase I chose was matte, I just drew
		the face I wanted on it with pencil (I could even erase!) and then traced
		with the black paint pen. If you're more confident in your artistic
		abilities, maybe you could spice it up with some additional colors! I just
		kept mine super simple since I knew the more I messed with it the worse it
		would look.
